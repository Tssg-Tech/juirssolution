Business Registration & Company Incorporation

Choosing the correct legal structure is one of the most important decisions when starting a business. Registration gives the business a legal identity, regulatory recognition, and credibility in the market.

Different business structures have different requirements regarding ownership, liability, management, and compliance.

We provide legal assistance for incorporation and registration of Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Private Limited Company, One Person Company (OPC), Trust, Society, and NGOs.

1. Proprietorship Firm

A Proprietorship Firm is the simplest form of business owned and controlled by a single individual. There is no separate legal identity, and the owner is personally liable for all business obligations.

Members
  • Minimum – 1 Owner
  • Maximum – 1 Owner
Time Required

3 – 7 days

Documents Required
  • PAN Card of proprietor
  • Aadhaar Card / Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Business Address Proof
  • Passport Size Photograph
  • Bank Account Details

2. Partnership Firm

A Partnership Firm is formed when two or more persons agree to carry on business together and share profits. It is governed by the Indian Partnership Act, 1932.

The relationship between partners is defined by a Partnership Deed which includes rights, duties, and profit-sharing ratio.

Members
  • Minimum – 2 Partners
  • Maximum – 50 Partners
Time Required

5 – 10 days

Key Document

Partnership Deed

Documents Required
  • PAN Card of partners
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Partnership Deed
  • Office Address Proof

3. Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

LLP combines the flexibility of a partnership with the benefits of limited liability. Governed by LLP Act, 2008.

Members
  • Minimum – 2 Partners
  • Minimum – 2 Designated Partners
  • At least one must be an Indian Resident
  • No Maximum Limit
Time Required

10 – 15 days

Key Document

LLP Agreement

Documents Required
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar / Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Photographs
  • Registered Office Proof
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)

4. Private Limited Company

A Private Limited Company is a separate legal entity under Companies Act, 2013. It is widely used for startups due to limited liability and investment benefits.

Directors
  • Minimum – 2
  • Maximum – 15
Shareholders
  • Minimum – 2
  • Maximum – 200
Time Required

10 – 20 days

Key Documents
  • Memorandum of Association (MOA)
  • Articles of Association (AOA)
Documents Required
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Address Proof
  • Photographs
  • Registered Office Proof
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Director Identification Number (DIN)

5. One Person Company (OPC)

OPC allows a single entrepreneur to operate with limited liability under Companies Act, 2013.

  • Minimum Members – 1
  • Maximum Members – 1
  • Directors – 1 to 15
  • Time – 10 – 15 days
Key Documents

MOA & AOA

Documents Required
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Address Proof
  • Photograph
  • Registered Office Proof
  • DSC

6. Trust Registration

A Trust is created for charitable, religious, or social purposes.

  • Minimum – 2 Trustees
  • No Maximum Limit
  • Time – 7 – 15 days
Key Document

Trust Deed

Documents Required
  • PAN Card
  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Photographs
  • Trust Deed
  • Office Proof

7. Society Registration

A Society is formed for charitable, educational, and social purposes under the Societies Registration Act, 1860.

  • Minimum – 7 Members
  • No Maximum Limit
  • Time – 15 – 25 days
Key Documents
  • Memorandum of Association (MOA)
  • Rules & Regulations
Documents Required
  • PAN & ID Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Photographs
  • MOA
  • Rules
  • Office Proof

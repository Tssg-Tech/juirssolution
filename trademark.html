Trademark Registration

A Trademark is a distinctive sign, symbol, logo, word, phrase, design, or combination that identifies and distinguishes the goods or services of one business from another.

  • Brand names
  • Company names
  • Logos
  • Taglines
  • Product names
  • Symbols / identifiers

Governed by the Trade Marks Act, 1999. Valid for 10 years and renewable indefinitely.
